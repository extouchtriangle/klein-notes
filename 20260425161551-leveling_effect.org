#+title: Leveling Effect
[[id:1a10a19d-430a-456c-ba63-57845d697b04][Acids/Bases]]
* Leveling Effect
- Strongest possible acid/base in solution is the conjugate acid/base of the solvent
- Aprotic solvents like hexane and tetrahydrofuran (THF) are used for strong bases
